FAQ's

1- What’s Jobpass ?

Jobpass is a website where you can create your own profile and update it with your personal and work experience. A CV look and feel will be generated upon completing all necessary information. You will be able to manage your profile after that and amend any information if you wish

2- How can I register to Jobpass?

Through Register Tab, you can set your desired User Name as well as Password and then your account will be created

3- What are the needed details while creating a new profile?

users are requested to add below details:
Basic Information
Projects Information
Work Experience
Skills Information
Language Information

4- Can I edit any information after adding it?

Yes, you can edit and even delete if you wish only by selecting Manage Profile Tab, then you need to edit the part you wish and complete the action

5- How many projects or skills I can add?

You can add as much as you wish. It’s unlimited

6- How can you reach out to me if you need a support?

You can approach me through Contact Me tab. All you need to do is sending me a message and I’ll respond back
